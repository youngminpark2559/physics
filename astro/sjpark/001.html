So, moon looks new moon shape # ====================================================================== When sun starts to set at 18:00, waxing crescent moon is located in highest southern area Sun light lays down onto moon from side and far direction of the sun So, waxing crescent moon brights in half Waxing crescent moon starts to rise at 12:00 when sun is in highest southern area but waxing crescent moon is not bright at 12:00 because sky is too bright due to existing sun Waxing crescent moon starts to show at 18:00 when sun starts to set Waxing crescent moon sets at 00:00 So, when army builds strategy where army wants to have moon light when they move, and army doesn't want to have moon light when they start to attack, army needs to choose period when waxing crescent moon shows. Specifically, 7 or 8th day as lunar calendar # ====================================================================== Note that, new moon can't show in highest southern area in the sky as if waxing crescent moon is located at 18:00. Since new moon is located in near to to sun, when sun disappears, new moon also disappears after showing in short time at early evening and night So, specifically, you can't see new moon after 22:00 If you read novel where 2 samurais duel at 00:00 with having new moon in the sky, it doesn't actually make sense because new moon can't show after 22:00 # ====================================================================== Full moon When sun sets, moon is located in opposite position to sun So, full moon starts to show when sun starts to set Then full moon shows high in the night Full moon sets when sun rises in the morning So, if you need to move in the night with moon light, you need to choose period when full moon shows # ====================================================================== Waning moon Waning moon rises and starts to show at 00:00 When sun rises at 6:00, waning moon is located in highest southern area (but waning moon is not bright moon due to sun) At 12:00, not bright waning moon sets # ====================================================================== Old moon In old moon peirod, there is no moon in the night sky. When sun starts to show at 6:00, old moon shows for short time, and soon, it becomes not bright moon in the sky
